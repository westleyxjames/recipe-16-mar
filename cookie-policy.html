Cookie Policy

Last Updated: March 12, 2026

1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how FlavorSprout ("we," "us," or "our") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies when you visit our website. This policy explains what these technologies are, why we use them, and your rights to control our use of them.

By using our website, you agree to the use of cookies and similar technologies as described in this Cookie Policy. If you do not agree, you should adjust your browser settings or discontinue use of our website.

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. Cookies are widely used by website owners to make their websites work more efficiently and to provide reporting information.

Cookies set by the website owner (in this case, FlavorSprout) are called "first-party cookies." Cookies set by parties other than the website owner are called "third-party cookies." Third-party cookies enable features or functionality to be provided on or through the website (e.g., analytics, advertising).

3. Why We Use Cookies

We use first-party and third-party cookies for several reasons:

  • Essential Website Functions: Some cookies are required for technical reasons for our website to operate
  • User Experience: To remember your preferences and settings
  • Analytics: To understand how visitors use our website and identify areas for improvement
  • Advertising: To deliver relevant advertisements to you
  • Security: To protect our website and your information from unauthorized access

4. Types of Cookies We Use

4.1 Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for you to browse our website and use its features. Without these cookies, services you have requested (such as remembering your cookie consent preferences) cannot be provided.

Examples:

  • Cookie consent status
  • Security cookies for authentication
  • Load balancing cookies

Duration: Session or up to 1 year

4.2 Functionality Cookies

These cookies allow our website to remember choices you make (such as your language preference) and provide enhanced, more personalized features.

Examples:

  • Language preferences
  • Region preferences
  • Font size and display preferences
  • Remembering if you've subscribed to our newsletter

Duration: Up to 2 years

4.3 Performance and Analytics Cookies

These cookies collect information about how you use our website, such as which pages you visit most often and if you receive error messages. This information helps us improve how our website works.

Examples include:

  • Google Analytics cookies
  • Page load time tracking
  • Error reporting
  • Traffic source analysis

Duration: Up to 2 years

4.4 Advertising and Targeting Cookies

These cookies are used to deliver advertisements that are relevant to you and your interests. They may also be used to limit the number of times you see an advertisement and help measure the effectiveness of advertising campaigns.

Examples:

  • Google AdSense cookies
  • Facebook Pixel
  • Retargeting cookies
  • Interest-based advertising

Duration: Up to 2 years

4.5 Social Media Cookies

These cookies enable you to share content from our website on social media platforms. They may also be used by social media platforms to track your browsing behavior across different websites.

Examples:

  • Facebook social plugins
  • Instagram embeds
  • Pinterest Pin It buttons
  • Twitter sharing widgets

Duration: Session to 2 years

5. Specific Cookies We Use

Below is a detailed list of the cookies we use on our website:

First-Party Cookies

  • cookieConsent - Stores your cookie consent preferences (1 year)
  • sessionId - Maintains your session while browsing (session)
  • userPreferences - Stores your display and feature preferences (1 year)

Third-Party Cookies

  • _ga, _gid (Google Analytics) - Track and analyze website traffic (2 years / 24 hours)
  • _fbp (Facebook Pixel) - Track conversions and build targeted audiences (3 months)
  • IDE, DSID (Google Advertising) - Serve targeted advertisements (13 months / 2 weeks)
  • fr (Facebook) - Enable ad delivery and measurement (3 months)

6. How Long Do Cookies Last?

Cookies can remain on your device for different periods of time:

  • Session Cookies: These are temporary cookies that are deleted when you close your browser
  • Persistent Cookies: These remain on your device until they expire or you delete them. We use persistent cookies for various durations, from 24 hours to 2 years, depending on their purpose

7. Third-Party Cookies and Services

We use several third-party services that may set cookies on your device:

7.1 Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how our website is used. Google Analytics collects information anonymously and reports website trends without identifying individual visitors.

Learn more: Google Privacy Policy

7.2 Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. These ads may be personalized based on your browsing behavior.

Learn more: Google Advertising

7.3 Social Media Platforms

We integrate social media sharing buttons from platforms like Facebook, Instagram, Pinterest, and Twitter. These platforms may set cookies to track your interactions.

8. How to Control Cookies

8.1 Cookie Consent Banner

When you first visit our website, you'll see a cookie consent banner. You can choose to:

  • Accept All: Allow all cookies
  • Decline: Decline non-essential cookies (essential cookies will still be used)

8.2 Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can:

  • Block all cookies
  • Block only third-party cookies
  • Delete cookies when you close your browser
  • Clear existing cookies

8.3 Browser-Specific Instructions

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Preferences > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings > Cookies and site permissions > Cookies and site data

8.4 Opt-Out Tools

You can opt out of certain types of cookies using these tools:

  • Google Analytics Opt-out: Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on
  • Interest-Based Advertising: NAI Opt-out or DAA Opt-out

9. Impact of Blocking Cookies

If you choose to block or delete cookies, some features of our website may not function properly:

  • You may need to re-enter your preferences each visit
  • Some pages may not load correctly
  • Personalized features may not work
  • We won't be able to remember your cookie consent choice
  • You may still see advertisements, but they won't be tailored to your interests

10. Do Not Track Signals

Some browsers have a "Do Not Track" (DNT) feature that lets you tell websites that you do not want to have your online activities tracked. Currently, our website does not respond to DNT browser settings or signals. We will update this policy if we implement DNT signal recognition in the future.

11. Mobile Device Identifiers

When you access our website via a mobile device, we may collect a unique device identifier or advertising identifier. These identifiers serve similar purposes to cookies. You can manage or reset these identifiers through your device settings.

Managing Mobile Identifiers:

  • iOS: Settings > Privacy > Advertising > Limit Ad Tracking
  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization

12. Cookies and Children

Our website is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with information, please contact us immediately.

13. International Users

Our website is hosted in the United States. If you are visiting from the European Union or other regions with data protection laws, please note that cookies will transfer your data to the United States. By using our website, you consent to this transfer.

13.1 EU Users and GDPR

If you are located in the European Union, you have certain rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) regarding cookies and personal data. You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Rectify inaccurate data
  • Erase your data
  • Restrict processing
  • Object to processing
  • Data portability
